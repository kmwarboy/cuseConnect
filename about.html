cuse connect...

is an app designed to allow residents within the City of Syracuse to easily access important residential information including waste removal, recieve updates on service requests and upcoming maintenance near their residence, and file service requests with the City of Syracuse. We hope you enjoy its ease of use, and convenience!

why?

Syracuse has started to become a more modern city recent years. As one of the largest metropolitan areas in the state, I felt there was a need for smarter access to information for city residents. There is a growing tech community, and a demand for a more modern lifestyle.

one quick thing...

In order to give you the most accurate information, you will be asked for your home address when you sign up. This allows us to serve you better, and allows you to access and deliver information more easily.
